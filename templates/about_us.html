About Us

Our Mission

To bring delicious meals from your favorite canteen directly to your fingertips, making ordering simple and fast.

Our Team

A passionate group of foodies and tech enthusiasts committed to improving your dining experience.

Our Values

Quality, speed, and customer satisfaction are at the core of everything we do at Ziggy Eats.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Ziggy Eats was born out of a love for food and technology. Our goal was simple: make ordering food from your campus canteen as easy as possible. Today, we're proud to serve hundreds of students daily with fresh and fast meals.

  • Real-time order tracking
  • Exclusive discounts and offers
  • Wide variety of canteen options
